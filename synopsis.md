VERSION 1 — THE PITCH SYNOPSIS
For a festival submission or Netflix one-sheet

THE UNCERTAIN ONES
A documentary film

The AI revolution didn't come with a warning. It came with a ChatGPT login and a quiet dread that spread from boardrooms to bedrooms — the suspicion that the skills you spent years building might not matter anymore.
In the middle of that uncertainty, 34 strangers enrolled in a course.
They came from everywhere. Coders and designers. HR managers and career-switchers. People who'd never written a line of code and senior engineers who suddenly felt behind. Some came chasing opportunity. Some came running from fear. Most couldn't tell the difference.
The Uncertain Ones tracks 30 of them across six months — their screens, their doubts, their 2am breakthroughs, and the friendships nobody planned for. What begins as a curriculum becomes something harder to name. A mirror. A reckoning. A second chance at a first idea.
Not all of them ship. Not all of them pivot. But every single one of them changes — in ways the course never promised and the brochure couldn't have predicted.
This is not a film about artificial intelligence.
It's about what happens to very human people when the world stops feeling certain.
